Tony Starks

The man that made the franchise

Here's a time line of Tony Stark:

  • 1970 (May 29th) - Born in Manhattan, New York City to Howard and Maria Stark, both influential figures in the scientific and business world.
  • 1980s - Loses his parents in a car accident orchestrated by Winter Soldier (revealed later in Captain America: Civil War).
  • Late 1980s - Early 1990s - Graduates from MIT with a degree in engineering and physics. Takes over Stark Industries after his father's death. Becomes known as a playboy billionaire and weapons manufacturer.
  • 1991 - Witnesses the devastating impact of Stark Industries' weapons firsthand during a military demonstration in Afghanistan.
  • 2008 - Captured by terrorists while showcasing new weaponry in Afghanistan. Severely injured by shrapnel, he builds the first Iron Man suit to escape captivity.
  • 2008 (Later) - Publicly reveals himself as Iron Man and vows to use his technology for good. Battles his former mentor, Obadiah Stane, who covets his Iron Man technology.
  • 2010 - Encounters Whiplash, a disgruntled scientist seeking revenge on Stark Industries. Develops the War Machine suit for James Rhodes and establishes himself as a global peacekeeper.
  • 2012 - Joins forces with Captain America, Thor, Hulk, Black Widow, and Hawkeye to form the Avengers and defend New York City from an alien invasion led by Loki.
  • 2013 - Struggles with PTSD from the events of the alien invasion. Faces the Mandarin, a terrorist leader who uses stolen Stark technology.
  • 2015 - Creates Ultron, a powerful AI designed to protect Earth, but Ultron turns rogue and the Avengers must work together to stop him.
  • 2016 - Supports government oversight of superheroes in the Sokovia Accords, leading to a rift in the Avengers team. Fights Captain America and his allies who oppose the Accords.
  • 2017 - Mentors Peter Parker, a young man with newfound spider-like abilities.
  • 2018 - Thanos, a powerful intergalactic warlord, arrives on Earth seeking the Infinity Stones. Despite the Avengers' best efforts, Thanos succeeds in wiping out half of all life in the universe with a snap of his fingers.
  • 2023 - The surviving Avengers successfully travel through time and acquire the Infinity Stones from the past to reverse Thanos' actions. Tony Stark sacrifices himself by wielding the Infinity Stones to defeat Thanos, saving the universe.
